About Me

Phillip Besse was born and raised on Cape Cod. He spent his summers in and on the ocean around the Cape & Islands. He quickly graduated to “blue water” travel, frequently spending weeks at sea dozens of miles from shore. An avid mariner, he obtained his Master 100 Ton USCG Captain’s License. He has 30,000 nm logged.


In 2016 he moved back to Cape Cod after teaching in Boston and met Estelle, the love of his life in - of all places - a tattoo shop. They married in 2019. In 2020 they welcomed their daughter Ella. Their son Keith followed in 2021.


Phillip Besse is new to web design but is enjoying the challenge. He finds it incredibly rewarding when a long time spent experimenting delivers the desired results. He looks forward to the rest of the coding bootcamp.
